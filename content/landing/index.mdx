## What is a Hackerspace?

A hackerspace is a community-operated physical space where people can meet and work on their projects. It provides access to tools, resources, and a collaborative environment for innovation and creativity.

---

## What is Hacking?

Defining hacking can be challenging due to its variable nature. However, a common trait among many hackers is their playful, intelligent, and exploratory mindset.
